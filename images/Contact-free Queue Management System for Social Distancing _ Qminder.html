Keep your locations safely open during COVID-19 quarantine

Safe visits, planned ahead

Let people see your peak hours and visitor capacity. Customers will make decisions on when to visit, based on concrete service data. Use queue management to avoid crowded waiting rooms from the first step.

Virtual visitor queuing

Help your customers practice social distancing, by giving them the means to self check in remotely. Let customers sign in by a call, online or by scanning a QR code and then wait from the safety of their homes or cars.

Connect from a safe distance

Send SMS messages to visitors with status updates. Share any changes or delays in service over the phone to prepare for service and minimize visit time.

The best time to visit

Customers check, in real time, the wait times and visitor capacity of your locations. This allows to avoid peak hours and reduce the burden on your staff.

Remote sign-in

Customers join a virtual queue, without the need to be physically present at the location.

Chat with customers

Customers get status updates and other relevant information from service clerks via SMS messages.

Location visit

Customers only visit the location once they’re called in. This eliminates the need for crowded lobbies and helps practice social distancing.

Greet & serve

Customers receive service, according to the general safety guidelines.
